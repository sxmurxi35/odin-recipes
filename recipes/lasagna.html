lasagna

This lasagna, made with ground beef and whole wheat noodles, is a comforting and nutritious dish that’s sure to satisfy the whole family. Packed with flavor and a healthier twist, it’s perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 12 whole wheat lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 2 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano, or to taste
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 (16 ounce) package cottage cheese
  • ½ cup shredded Parmesan cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 4½ cups tomato-basil pasta sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Boil salted water, cook lasagna noodles for 10 minutes or until al dente, then drain.
  3. Cook ground beef, garlic, oregano, garlic powder, salt, and pepper in a skillet over medium heat until browned, about 10 minutes.
  4. Mix cottage cheese, Parmesan cheese, and eggs together in a large bowl until thoroughly combined.
  5. Layer 4 noodles, sauce, beef mixture, and cottage cheese in a 9x13-inch pan. Repeat twice, ending with sauce, then top with mozzarella. Cover with foil.
  6. Bake at 350°F for 30 minutes until bubbly. Remove foil and bake 10 more minutes until cheese browns. Let stand 10 minutes before serving.
